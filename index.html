Just like any Video Game, they're rules to this quiz!
When starting the Video Game Quiz:
1. You'll have a total of 1 minute answer questions. But there's a catch to the quiz...
2. Once question is answered there is no turning back.
3. Answering the question correctly wont affect the timer.
4. Answering the question wrong will deduct 10 seconds.
5. Once you're done with the quiz enter you're initials to show off your high score!
